**Advisor:** Dr. Ikram Syed.
**Era:** Spring 2018.

Built an SVM-based sentiment-classification pipeline over Pakistani social-media posts to analyze public sentiment during the 2018 general election. Model predictions tracked observed polling patterns, providing a proof-of-concept for ML-on-social-text applied to regional political analysis. Early exposure to NLP that informed later thinking about text-image grounding in vision-language work.
