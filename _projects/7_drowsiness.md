**Setting:** Undergraduate ML project.
**Era:** 2017–2018.

Trained a classifier to distinguish open vs. closed eyes from video frames, accumulating a drowsiness score over time and triggering an alarm once the score crossed a threshold. Motivated by the US Department of Transportation statistic that human error accounts for ~90% of vehicle accidents. The human-state-from-biosignals motif here later reappeared in the PhD work on wearable stress detection (MuSDeT, MULA@CVPR 2026).
