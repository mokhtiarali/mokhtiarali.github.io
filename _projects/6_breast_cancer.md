**Setting:** B.S. Software Engineering capstone / early deep-learning exploration.
**Era:** 2017–2018.

Trained a convolutional neural network on IDC histopathology image datasets to predict whether tiles contain Invasive Ductal Carcinoma. Used an 80/20 split, augmentation for class imbalance, and explored depth vs. accuracy trade-offs on limited-compute hardware. This early medical-imaging exposure foreshadowed the later PhD direction toward multimodal physiological fusion (MICCAI 2026).
