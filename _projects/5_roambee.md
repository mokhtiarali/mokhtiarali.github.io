**Advisor:** Dr. Kaiqun Fu (Kai Group / Decklar).
**Duration:** Fall 2023 – Spring 2024.

Analyzed transportation data collected via Roambee's IoT sensors using geospatial and graph-theoretic techniques. Focused on lane-specific journey details to optimize routes and identify low-coverage regions through dataset clustering. Produced a geospatial pipeline that cleaned, georeferenced, and clustered journey traces at scale, surfacing coverage gaps that were not visible in raw sensor dashboards.
