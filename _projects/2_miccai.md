**Venue:** MICCAI 2026 (under review).
**Role:** Co-first author (with Seungpil Choi).

A framework for fusing heterogeneous physiological signals under uncertainty, including an auditing step that flags unreliable predictions before downstream use. The work contributes a representation-learning objective that decouples modality-specific and shared components, paired with a trustworthiness score for clinical-adjacent decision making.
